Storyline

*Gyaarah Gyaarah* is a crime-thriller series blending science fiction with mystery. The story revolves around two police officers, Shaurya Anthwal from the 1990s and Yug Arya from 2016, who are connected through a supernatural walkie-talkie. This device, which activates for 60 seconds at exactly 11:11 pm, allows them to communicate across time and collaboratively solve complex cases. As they work together, they unravel a larger conspiracy that spans multiple decades, involving hidden truths and the consequences of altering events in the past. The narrative explores themes of time, fate, and justice in a thrilling and suspenseful way.
